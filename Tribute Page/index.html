Shushant Singh Rajput - Gem of Indian Cinema

About the Captain of Bollywood

♦️ Shushant Singh Rajput was born on 21 January, 1986 to Krishna Kumar Singh and Usha Singh in Patna, Bihar, India.

♦️ He attended the St. Karen's High School in Patna. His family moved to Delhi following his mother's death in 2002 where Shushant completed his schooling for intermediate studies in Kulachi Hansraj Model School.

♦️ Shushant was reportedly an avid reader who was interested in astrophysics and won the National Olympiad in Physics. He secured admission in the Delhi College of Engineering to pursue B.Tech. in Mechanical Engineering.

♦️ According to Shushant, he did not have any interest in engineering but his family gave him no option which left him dissatisfied. He instead wanted to become an astronaut and later an air force pilot but was also interested in Bollywood, being a fan of Shah Rukh Khan.

♦️ During his course at the college, Shushant enrolled himself in Shiamak Davar's dance classes. He featured as a background dancer in the 'Dhoom Again' song of "DHOOM 2" and in Aishwarya Rai's performance at the closing ceremony of 2006 Commonwealth Games.

♦️ In 2006, he dropped out of college during his fourth year to begin his career in arts and performance. He moved to Mumbai, took up odd jobs and small roles alongside working in the theatre industry. He then joined Nadira Babbar's theatre group called 'Ekjute' in which he remained for two and a half years.

♦️ He was spotted by a casting team of Blaji Telefilms and subsequently called for an audition and cast as a second lead in the TV series 'Kis Desh Mei Hai Mera Dil'. His performance had made an impression on the producer Ekta Kapoor.

♦️ Ekta Kapoor then vouched for him to be cast as the lead in another series 'Pavitra Rishta', reportedly against the wishes of the hosting channel, Zee TV. The role was reportedly a breakthrough for him and for which he won various television awards, including his first award for best actor in the Indian Television Academy Awards.

♦️ He made his Bollywood debut in Abhishek Kapoor's "Kai Po Che!" in 2013. This film became a critical and commercial success. Shushant received a nomination for the Filmfare Awards in the category of best debut actor for his performance in the film.

♦️ He then Featured into many movies as Rajkumar Hirani's Aamir Khan starrer "PK", Yash Raj Films produced "Shudh Desi Romance", YRF's and Dibakar Banarjee's "Detective Byomkesh Bakshi".

♦️ In 2014, Rajput was announced as the actor who would play the role of Dhoni in the biopic which was titled "M.S. Dhoni: The Untold Story", directed by Neeraj Pandey. It was released on 2 October, 2016 and became the 5th highest grossing Bollywood film of that year. He received critical acclaim for his performance and nominations to a number of awards.

♦️ In 2018, he was signed for Nitesh Tiwari's "Chhichhore". This movie was released on 6 September 2019, and became the third highest movie to gross over ₹100 crore (equivalent to ₹124 crore).

♦️ On 14 June 2020, Shushant, aged 34, was found dead, hanging fron the ceiling fan in his home in Bandra, Mumbai. He had reportedly shown signs of clinical depression and had bipolar disorder. The Mumbai Police commenced an investigation, stating that the death was being treated as suicide.
